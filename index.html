OUR STORY

U MÊ KAPE — Vietnamese coffee in every moment

U MÊ KAPE was born from a love of slow mornings, roasted beans, and the warm feeling of gathering together. Every detail in our space is designed to feel familiar, cozy, and full of story.

From the first café to the scenes of friends crossing paths, our story is written one cup at a time. This is the place where tradition meets a modern take on Vietnamese coffee culture.

Milestones

Our journey as a Vietnamese-inspired coffee destination in Manila, serving students, professionals, and coffee lovers in the heart of Taft Avenue.

2024

Discovered by the Community

Located in Malate, Manila beside the university district, U ME KAPE began gaining attention among students and café enthusiasts for its Vietnamese coffee offerings, cozy interiors, and study-friendly atmosphere. Community discussions started highlighting its signature Egg Coffee and authentic Vietnamese café experience.

2025

Growing Online Presence

U ME KAPE became increasingly visible through social media recommendations and café discovery platforms. Customers praised the café's late-night operating hours, Vietnamese-inspired menu, and laptop-friendly environment, helping it build a loyal customer base among students and remote workers.

2025

Recognized as a Study & Work Hub

The café was featured on café directories highlighting work-friendly spaces in Metro Manila. Reviews emphasized its free Wi-Fi, comfortable seating, welcoming ambiance, and authentic Vietnamese food and beverage offerings, making it a preferred destination for studying and remote work.

2026

Established Taft Destination

Operating from the Manor Building along Taft Avenue, U ME KAPE continues to serve as a 24-hour coffee destination in Malate. The café is recognized for Vietnamese-style coffee, particularly its Egg Coffee, and remains a popular stop for students from nearby universities and Manila café explorers.
